Valorant

Valorant is a team-based FPS game where players choose unique Agents with special abilities. The goal is to plant or defuse a bomb while outsmarting and eliminating the opposing team. Tactical gameplay, teamwork, and precise gunplay are key to victory.

Agents

Duelist

Duelists are aggressive agents who excel in initiating fights and taking down enemies. They often have abilities that allow them to close the distance quickly or engage in one-on-one duels with high damage output.

Initiator

Initiators are agents who excel at gathering information and initiating team fights. They have abilities that reveal enemy locations or disrupt their positioning, allowing their team to gain an advantage when engaging in combat.

Sentinal

Sentinels are defensive agents who focus on protecting their team and holding down specific areas on the map. They have abilities that create defensive barriers, heal teammates, or provide early warning systems, making them crucial for maintaining map control and supporting their allies.

Controller

Controllers specialize in manipulating the battlefield and controlling the flow of the game. They have abilities that create barriers, smoke screens, or zones of control, enabling them to block off areas or limit the enemy's movement.
